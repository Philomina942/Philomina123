Recipe: Egg Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese
  • Optional: chopped tomatoes, mushrooms, spinach

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl. Add the milk, salt, and pepper. Whisk until well combined.
  2. Heat the butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and bell pepper. Cook until softened.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet. Let it cook for a minute without stirring.
  5. Gently lift the edges with a spatula and tilt the pan to let the uncooked eggs flow underneath.
  6. When the eggs are almost set, sprinkle the grated cheese and any additional toppings over one half of the omelette.
  7. Fold the other half over the toppings. Cook for another minute, then slide the omelette onto a plate.

Notes

Serve immediately with toast or a side salad. Enjoy your delicious egg omelette!
